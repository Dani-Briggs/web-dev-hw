Briefly describe one way how data travels on the internet.

Data is sent through the internet in Binary code (1s and 0s).
This code can travel through the internet in three ways. One way is through WiFi.
Wifi is basically radio waves that can connect to other devices in a short range.

What is the relationship between an IP address and the DNS?

The DNS helps your device connect to other IP addresses. The DNS is like your Uber driver for getting your
device to website to website.

What is a cookie?

A cookie is data the websites use to remember who you are. These are
usually for websites that you can make an account on. 

List the three basic parts of a URL.
